5 scoops of cookie batter

Ingredient
butter 113g           807cal
salt 1.5g
sugar powder 25g
chopped green onion 10g
cake flour 150g             527cal

1. BEAT and MIX
Softened butter + sugar + salt

2. SIFT and ADD and BEAT
cake flour + Green onion

preheat 180C
